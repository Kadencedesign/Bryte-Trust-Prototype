It sounds like your team member may be going through a difficult time. Here are some ways to support them while staying within your role as a manager:

  • Check in privately: A low-pressure 1:1 — "I've noticed you seem a bit off lately, just wanted to see how you're doing" — can go a long way.
  • Share available resources: Remind them about your EAP (Employee Assistance Program) if your company offers one. It's confidential.
  • Adjust expectations temporarily: If you can offer flexibility on deadlines or workload, that may help while they stabilize.
  • Loop in HR if needed: If you're concerned about their wellbeing or the situation affecting the team, HR can advise on next steps without requiring a formal action.
